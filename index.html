Poem

By the Beach, I Find My Peace

By the beach, I find my peace,
Sitting in the golden sands, at ease.
Listening to the waves, their gentle song,
A lullaby for hearts, where we all belong.

The wind whispers secrets in my ear,
Brushing my face, so cool and clear.
It carries away my worries and care,
Leaving me with solace, a soul laid bare.

With each crashing wave, I'm set free,
All that I've known, it takes with glee.
In this moment, nature's sweet embrace,
I find serenity, in this tranquil place.

The sun kisses the horizon goodnight,
Painting the sky in hues of warm light.
As stars twinkle above, a celestial embrace,
I cherish this peace, by the beach's grace.

So here I'll stay, by the ocean's side,
Where the waves and the wind are my guide.
In this tranquil moment, I find my release,
By the beach, listening, finding inner peace.
